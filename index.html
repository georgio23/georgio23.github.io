About

Hello :) I am a third year PhD researcher based in University College Dublin, Ireland. I am interested in a diverse range of topics, but my current focus is on quantum sensing and nano electronic devices. I've also a keen interest in quantum transport and open quantum systems. Previously, I have completed my MSc in Applied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics (1st class honours, October 2020) in University College Dublin and BSc in Physics with Biomedical Sciences (1st class honours, September 2019) in Dublin City University. Outside of physics I enjoy diving (qualified divemaster), photography, and all out-doors activities :D

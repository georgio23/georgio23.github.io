Podcast: The Science Talk

Title: Quantum Thermometers.

Host: Dr. Pranoti Kshirsagar,

About video: In this video we discuss generally about quantum technologies, ranging from applications in computing to sensing. We discuss about life as a scientist, and how one can go about becoming one! This is not a technical talk and is aimed at a general audience: so if you are interested in quantum technologies, some of the struggles of doing a PhD, and more generally about some of my early work, please take a look and enjoy!
